About Conscient Vista Arrozal

Conscient Vista Arrozal is a premium residential project located in Candolim Goa. The project features smartly designed 2, 3, and 4 BHK apartments for high-end living. The landscaped areas and sit-out spaces are filled with tropical plants, art objects, and soothing music, creating an ambiance that reflects the Spanish-Goan Garden of Eden theme. Relax in the sit-out areas with uninterrupted views of the lush paddy fields and dense tree cover along the Nerul river. The community spaces at Vista Arrozal Goa have Spanish-style plazas with gardens and fountains. The existing chapel has been seamlessly integrated into the property, adding a unique and heavenly touch. Vista Arrozal clubhouse offers premium amenities like concierge services, a gymnasium, yoga room, an outdoor kids play area, a swimming pool and many more.
